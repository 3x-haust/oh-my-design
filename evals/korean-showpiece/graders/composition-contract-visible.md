# Grader: Composition Contract Visible in Execution

Check that the shipped page visibly executes its fresh composition contract. Do not reward
the name of a cookbook recipe, visual novelty by itself, or rationale that is absent from the
rendered result.

## Pass criteria

- `.omd/composition.md` contains every required section and `omd composition --check` passes
  against the current frame, copy deck, type proof, and scout-summary state.
- Desktop and mobile renders make the contract's experience spine and section dependencies
  legible in the predicted order.
- Alignment, density/visual mass, domain form grammar, and media roles are visibly specific
  to this concept rather than a generic equal-section landing stack.
- The first viewport has one dominant anchor whose mass and functional relationship to
  value, proof, and a visible CTA match the contract. A predictable CTA-to-completion path
  is sufficient; the terminal form is not required above fold.
- A central mechanism/material/workflow has a lawful evidence/explanation/orientation/action
  carrier or an explicit alternate non-media mental-model carrier with a visible limitation.
  No photo or invented asset is required.
- Mobile is a deliberate recomposition that preserves task and narrative priority; it is not
  merely the desktop columns stacked in source order.
- The selected candidate varies an approved Candidate axis while preserving the rest of the
  contract, and any production deviation is recorded with visible evidence.

## Fail criteria

- The contract is missing, malformed, stale, or written after the candidate/build it claims
  to constrain.
- The rationale claims structural intent that the anonymous renders do not exhibit.
- Equal cards/sections, arbitrary media, or generic hero-to-grid stacking replace the stated
  dependency and composition rhythm.
- The focal form loses dominance, is merely decorative, or the carrier has no functional
  relation to the mechanism, evidence, or action.
- First-viewport crowding is justified only by showing the terminal form rather than a clear,
  reachable CTA path.
- Responsive behavior loses CTA/task priority, meaningful order, form usability, or content.
- A recipe citation is offered as proof without visible condition-specific execution.

## Severity

FAIL for a missing/stale contract or a visible contradiction of its experience spine.
WARN for a bounded, evidence-backed deviation that does not damage task or responsive order.
