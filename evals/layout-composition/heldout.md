# Held-out protocol

Use Scenario 01 as the development task. Keep Scenarios 02 and 03 sealed as held-out tasks.
A release evaluation runs all three once with fresh candidate and blind-review contexts.
A held-out scenario may move into development only when a new generic Korean task replaces
it and the replacement is frozen before results are observed. Preserve rubric version,
`1280x900`/`390x844` viewports, and judge inputs across comparisons; publish per-dimension
scores rather than a winner alone.
